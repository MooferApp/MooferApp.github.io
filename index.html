Welcome to Moofer!

Moofer allows you to access previously inaccessible features of VanMoof bikes.
Please note that currently only S3, X3, S2 (beta) and X2 (beta) bikes are supported.

Previously inaccessible

  • Region / max. speed selection including offroad mode
  • Assist mode 5
  • Manual shifting
  • Hidden bell sound(s)
  • Smart Speed - AI-driven adaptive assist level based on speed- Speedometer
  • Smart Range - AI-driven adaptive assist level based on battery level
  • Clean dashboard including speed-o-meter, current gear, estimated range left, battery percentage, a clock, and quick toggles
  • Siri Shortcut and IFTTT support
  • AI-calculated estimated range
  • Alarm Alert
  • Charge Alert
  • Discharge Alert
  • Theft checker
  • Jukebox
  • Full English, Dutch, German, French and Danish translations
  • Full light and dark mode
  • ...and more!

This app makes certain features of the bike accessible which may be illegal to use in certain jurisdictions. It’s the rider’s responsibility to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
