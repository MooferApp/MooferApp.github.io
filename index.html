Welcome to Moofer!

Moofer allows you to access previously inaccessible features of VanMoof bikes.

Feel free to download the app in the App Store, or on Google Play.


Feature roadmap

If you want to suggest any features that should be added to the app, please do not hesitate and shoot an e-mail. The current feature roadmap looks like this:

  • AI-driven adaptive assist level based on range

  • Monthly alert to check tire pressure

  • Jukebox loop mode

  • GPS ride tracking

  • Sound based ride navigation

  • Sound volume control

  • Smartwatch support

  • Support for Siri Shortcuts

  • Support for older electrified / smart models

  • Widgets

  • Apple HealthKit and Google Fit support

  • ... and more!
